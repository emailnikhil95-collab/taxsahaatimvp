# Image Prompts: Investor & Freelancer Domain

## 1. Capital Gains
1. `short-term-capital-gains-tax-shares`
   - **Image Prompt:** A minimalist illustration of a stock market chart trending upwards, with a calendar showing a short duration (e.g., less than 12 months) and a tax calculation symbol.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 111A.

2. `ltcg-tax-mutual-funds-india`
   - **Image Prompt:** A graphic showing a mutual fund portfolio growing over a long timeline, with a shield or umbrella representing the ₹1.25 lakh tax exemption limit.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 112A.

3. `how-to-calculate-capital-gains-tax`
   - **Image Prompt:** A flat-design illustration of a calculator surrounded by icons of houses, stock certificates, and coins, with a step-by-step flowchart in the background.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax computation rules for capital gains.

4. `tax-on-sale-of-property-india`
   - **Image Prompt:** An illustration of a house with a "Sold" sign, transitioning into a pile of rupees, with a magnifying glass highlighting an indexation formula.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 48 (Indexation) and Section 54 series.

5. `set-off-capital-losses-against-gains`
   - **Image Prompt:** A balancing scale with a red downward arrow (loss) on one side and a green upward arrow (gain) on the other, achieving equilibrium.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 70 and 74.

6. `grandfathering-clause-ltcg-explained`
   - **Image Prompt:** A vintage clock or calendar pointing to January 31, 2018, acting as a protective barrier over a stack of stock certificates.
   - **Data Source:** Finance Act 2018, Section 112A grandfathering provisions.

7. `section-54-tax-exemption-property`
   - **Image Prompt:** A visual showing money flowing from an old, sold house directly into the construction of a new, modern house, bypassing a tax collector's net.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 54.

8. `capital-gains-on-espp-rsu`
   - **Image Prompt:** An illustration of a tech employee looking at a laptop screen showing company stock options (RSUs) vesting, with an Indian tax form in the foreground.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax rules for perquisites and capital gains on foreign shares.

9. `tax-on-foreign-stocks-india`
   - **Image Prompt:** A globe with a flight path connecting the US stock market (Wall Street bull) to an Indian tax filing document (Schedule FA).
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax rules for foreign assets and Section 112/111A equivalents.

10. `schedule-al-high-income-itr`
    - **Image Prompt:** A detailed checklist or ledger showing high-value assets (car, house, jewelry, stocks) with a stamp of approval, symbolizing Schedule AL compliance.
    - **Data Source:** ITR-2 and ITR-3 instructions for Schedule AL.

## 2. Futures & Options (F&O)
11. `f-and-o-trading-tax-india`
    - **Image Prompt:** A dual-screen trading setup showing fast-moving F&O charts, with a business ledger and tax forms neatly arranged on the desk.
    - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 43(5).

12. `is-f-and-o-business-income`
    - **Image Prompt:** A conceptual illustration showing a fork in the road: one path labeled "Capital Gains" (blocked) and the other "Business Income" (open), with a trader walking down the open path.
    - **Data Source:** Guidance Note on Tax Audit under Section 44AB of the Income-tax Act, 1961.

13. `how-to-file-itr-for-f-and-o-losses`
    - **Image Prompt:** A trader looking at a red portfolio on a screen, but holding a document that says "Carry Forward" with an arrow pointing to future tax years.
    - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 71 and 72.

14. `tax-audit-applicability-f-and-o`
    - **Image Prompt:** A magnifying glass hovering over a trading turnover calculation, with a CA's stamp of audit approval nearby.
    - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 44AB.

15. `itr-3-or-itr-4-for-f-and-o`
    - **Image Prompt:** A comparison graphic showing two doors, one labeled ITR-3 (open and welcoming for traders) and ITR-4 (with a warning sign for speculative/F&O).
    - **Data Source:** CBDT instructions for ITR forms.

16. `how-to-calculate-turnover-f-and-o`
    - **Image Prompt:** A mathematical illustration showing absolute profit and absolute loss being added together, symbolizing the F&O turnover calculation method.
    - **Data Source:** ICAI Guidance Note on Tax Audit.

    - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 115BBH.

18. `how-to-report-crypto-in-itr`

## 4. Freelancers & Professionals (Section 44ADA)
21. `itr-for-freelancers-india`
    - **Image Prompt:** A freelancer working on a laptop at a cafe, with floating icons of invoices, tax forms, and a checklist.
    - **Data Source:** General Income Tax rules for professionals.

22. `section-44ada-presumptive-taxation`
    - **Image Prompt:** A graphic showing a pie chart split exactly in half (50%), with one half labeled "Taxable Income" and the other "Presumed Expenses."
    - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act, Section 44ADA.

23. `professional-tax-itr-4`
    - **Image Prompt:** A clean, simplified tax form (ITR-4 Sugam) with a professional's tools (stethoscope, laptop, legal gavel) resting on it.

24. `44ada-eligible-professions-list`

27. `advance-tax-for-freelancers`

28. `tax-on-freelance-income-from-abroad`
