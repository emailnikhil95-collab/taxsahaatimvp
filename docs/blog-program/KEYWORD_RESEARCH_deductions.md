# Keyword Research: Deductions & Seniors

## Domain Overview
This domain covers tax-saving deductions available to Indian taxpayers, specifically focusing on Section 80C, Section 80D, House Rent Allowance (HRA), Home Loan interest/principal deductions, and special benefits for Senior Citizens.

## Core Search Themes & Intent

### 1. Section 80C (Investments & Expenses)
**Keywords:**
- "80c deduction list"
- "how to claim 80c in itr"
- "80c limit for ay 2026-27"
- "best 80c tax saving options"
- "elss vs ppf 80c"
- "epf deduction under 80c"
- "tuition fee 80c deduction"

**User Intent:** Taxpayers want to know what qualifies for the ₹1.5 lakh limit, how to maximize it, and how to report it when filing their ITR.

### 2. Section 80D (Health Insurance & Medical)
**Keywords:**
- "80d deduction limit"
- "80d medical bills for parents"
- "preventive health checkup 80d"
- "how to claim 80d in itr 1"
- "80d deduction for senior citizens"
- "cash payment for preventive health checkup"

**User Intent:** Users are looking to understand the limits (₹25k/₹50k), how to claim premiums paid for themselves and parents, and the rules around cash payments and preventive checkups.

### 3. House Rent Allowance (HRA)
**Keywords:**
- "hra exemption calculator"
- "how to claim hra if not in form 16"
- "hra and home loan together"
- "fake rent receipt for hra"
- "rent paid to parents hra"
- "hra exemption rules"

**User Intent:** Salaried employees want to calculate their exact exemption, know what to do if they forgot to submit proofs to their employer, and understand the legality of paying rent to family members or claiming HRA alongside a home loan.

### 4. Home Loan (Section 24b & 80EEA)
**Keywords:**
- "home loan tax benefit"
- "section 24b interest on housing loan"
- "80c principal repayment home loan"
- "joint home loan tax benefit"
- "under construction property tax benefit"
- "pre construction interest deduction"

**User Intent:** Homeowners want to maximize their tax savings by claiming both principal (80C) and interest (24b/80EEA), especially in cases of joint ownership or under-construction properties.

### 5. Senior Citizens (Special Benefits)
**Keywords:**
- "income tax for senior citizens"
- "80ttb deduction for senior citizens"
- "senior citizen tax slab ay 2026-27"
- "medical expenditure 80d senior citizen"
- "form 15h for senior citizens"
- "does senior citizen need to file itr"

**User Intent:** Senior citizens (and their children filing for them) want to understand special exemptions like 80TTB (interest income), higher basic exemption limits, and deductions for medical expenses without insurance.

## Content Strategy
- **Clarity over Jargon:** Explain sections using simple math and relatable examples (e.g., "If your salary is ₹10L and you invest ₹1.5L in PPF...").
- **Actionable Steps:** Show exactly where these go in the ITR forms.
- **Old vs. New Regime Context:** Always clarify that most of these deductions (80C, 80D, HRA, Home Loan on self-occupied) are ONLY available under the Old Tax Regime.
- **LastMinute ITR Integration:** Highlight how our platform helps auto-identify missing deductions or compares regimes to ensure they aren't losing money.
