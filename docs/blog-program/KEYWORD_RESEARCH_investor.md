# Keyword Research: Investor & Freelancer Domain

## Overview
This document outlines the primary search queries and keyword clusters for Indian taxpayers in the "Investor & Freelancer" domain. This domain covers Capital Gains (equity, mutual funds, property), Futures & Options (F&O), Cryptocurrency, Freelancing, and Presumptive Taxation (Section 44ADA).

## 1. Capital Gains (Equity, Mutual Funds, Property)
**Target Audience:** Retail investors, salaried individuals with stock market investments, property sellers.
**Primary Keywords:**
- short term capital gains tax on shares
- ltcg tax on mutual funds india
- how to calculate capital gains tax
- tax on sale of property in india
- set off capital losses against gains
- grandfathering clause ltcg
- tax exemption on sale of house property (Section 54)

**Search Intent:** Users want to know how much tax they owe on their investments, how to calculate it, and how to legally minimize it using exemptions or loss set-offs.

## 2. Futures & Options (F&O)
**Target Audience:** Active traders, day traders, individuals trading in derivatives.
**Primary Keywords:**
- f&o trading tax india
- is f&o business income or capital gains
- how to file itr for f&o losses
- tax audit applicability for f&o trading
- itr 3 or itr 4 for f&o
- how to calculate turnover for f&o

**Search Intent:** Traders are often confused about whether F&O is business income, which ITR form to use, how to report losses, and whether a tax audit is mandatory.

## 3. Cryptocurrency
**Target Audience:** Crypto investors and traders.
**Primary Keywords:**
- crypto tax in india 2026
- 30% tax on crypto
- how to report crypto in itr
- set off crypto losses india
- tds on crypto transactions
- 1% tds on vda

**Search Intent:** Users need clarity on the strict crypto tax rules, including the flat 30% rate, the inability to set off losses, and how to report Virtual Digital Assets (VDA) in their ITR.

## 4. Freelancers & Professionals (Section 44ADA)
**Target Audience:** Freelancers, consultants, doctors, lawyers, IT professionals.
**Primary Keywords:**
- itr for freelancers india
- section 44ada presumptive taxation
- professional tax itr 4
- 44ada eligible professions list
- how to claim expenses as freelancer
- itr 3 vs itr 4 for professionals
- gst for freelancers

**Search Intent:** Independent professionals want to simplify their tax filing, understand if they qualify for the 50% presumptive taxation scheme (44ADA), and know which ITR form to file.

## 5. General Investor & Freelancer Queries
**Primary Keywords:**
- advance tax for freelancers
- advance tax on capital gains
- how to reconcile ais with broker statement
- schedule al (assets and liabilities) for high income
- foreign stocks tax india (RSUs, ESPPs)

**Search Intent:** High-net-worth individuals or those with varied income streams looking for compliance guidance on advance tax, foreign assets, and AIS reconciliation.
