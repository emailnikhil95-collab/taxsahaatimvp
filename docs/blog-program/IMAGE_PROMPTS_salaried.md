# Image Prompts & Data Sources: Salaried & Regime Domain

1. `multiple-form-16-job-switch`
   - **Image Prompt:** A split-screen illustration showing two different office ID cards on the left, merging into a single clean tax document on the right, with a green checkmark.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act rules on combining salary from multiple employers.

2. `switch-tax-regime-at-itr-filing`
   - **Image Prompt:** A stylized toggle switch with "Old Regime" on one side and "New Regime" on the other, with a hand sliding the toggle towards a pile of saved coins.
   - **Data Source:** Section 115BAC provisions regarding the option to choose the tax regime at the time of filing the return.

3. `how-to-read-form-16`
   - **Image Prompt:** A magnifying glass hovering over a Form 16 document, highlighting specific sections like "Part A" and "Part B" in bright, easy-to-read colors.
   - **Data Source:** CBDT format for Form 16 Part A (TRACES) and Part B (Employer).

4. `file-itr-without-form-16`
   - **Image Prompt:** A puzzle being put together, with pieces labeled "Salary Slips," "Form 26AS," and "AIS," forming a complete picture of a tax return.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Department guidelines on filing returns using alternative documents when Form 16 is unavailable.

5. `claim-hra-forgot-proofs-employer`
   - **Image Prompt:** A calendar showing a missed deadline marked in red, with an arrow pointing to a laptop screen showing a successful tax filing with HRA claimed.
   - **Data Source:** Section 10(13A) of the Income Tax Act and related ITAT rulings allowing HRA claims at the time of filing.

6. `standard-deduction-new-vs-old-regime`
   - **Image Prompt:** A comparison graphic showing two scales: one labeled "Old Regime" with a ₹50,000 weight, and the other "New Regime" with a ₹75,000 weight.
   - **Data Source:** Finance Act 2025/2026 updates on standard deduction under Section 16(ia).

7. `notice-period-pay-taxability`
   - **Image Prompt:** A calendar with a "Notice Period" block highlighted, alongside a payslip showing a deduction, with a question mark over the tax calculation.
   - **Data Source:** Income Tax Act provisions on taxation of salary "due or received" and relevant case laws on notice period pay recovery.

8. `joining-bonus-recovery-tax`
   - **Image Prompt:** A boomerang returning to its sender, symbolizing a joining bonus being returned, with tax documents in the background.
   - **Data Source:** Section 15 and Section 89 of the Income Tax Act regarding taxation of salary and relief on recovery.

9. `leave-encashment-tax-exemption`
   - **Image Prompt:** A suitcase and a beach hat next to a stack of cash, representing unused vacation days converted into money.
   - **Data Source:** Section 10(10AA) of the Income Tax Act detailing leave encashment exemption limits.

10. `gratuity-tax-exemption-limit`
    - **Image Prompt:** A timeline showing 5 years of service, leading to a gift box labeled "Gratuity," with a shield symbolizing tax exemption.
    - **Data Source:** Section 10(10) of the Income Tax Act and the Payment of Gratuity Act.

11. `section-89-relief-salary-arrears`
    - **Image Prompt:** A calendar showing past years, with coins flowing from the past into the current year, and a protective umbrella labeled "Section 89."
    - **Data Source:** Section 89(1) of the Income Tax Act and Rule 21A.

12. `form-10e-filing-guide`
    - **Image Prompt:** A digital form labeled "10E" on a computer screen, with a step-by-step progress bar showing completion.
    - **Data Source:** Income Tax e-filing portal guidelines for submitting Form 10E.

13. `pf-withdrawal-taxability`
    - **Image Prompt:** A piggy bank with "EPF" written on it being opened, with a calendar showing less than 5 years, and a tax deduction symbol.
    - **Data Source:** Section 192A and Rule 8 of Part A of the Fourth Schedule to the Income Tax Act.

14. `report-perquisites-form-16-itr`
    - **Image Prompt:** Icons of a car, a house, and stock certificates, pointing towards a specific line item on a tax form.
    - **Data Source:** Section 17(2) of the Income Tax Act and Rule 3 defining perquisite valuation.

15. `relocation-allowance-taxability`
