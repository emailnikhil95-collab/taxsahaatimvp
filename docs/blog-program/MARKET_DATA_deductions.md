## Notes on figures

- **PPF 7.1% / EPF 8.25%:** small-savings rates are reset quarterly by the Ministry of Finance; EPF is declared annually by EPFO. Both are realistic for FY 2024-25 / FY 2025-26 but should be re-checked at filing time.
- **80C Rs 1.5L, 80D Rs 25k/50k, 80TTB Rs 50k, Section 24b Rs 2L, 80EEA Rs 1.5L:** statutory caps fixed in the Income Tax Act; stable across recent years.
- **Stamp duty 5-7%:** varies by state and by buyer category (e.g., women buyers often get a rebate); the 80C claim is always capped at Rs 1.5L combined.
- **Compliance:** all articles position LastMinute ITR as a filing companion only. The user files and e-verifies on incometax.gov.in. No claims of guaranteed refunds, filing on the user's behalf, government partnership, or auto-submission.
