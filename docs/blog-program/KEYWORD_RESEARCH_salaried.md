- Most guides on "multiple Form 16s" are too technical. We need a simple step-by-step guide on combining incomes and recalculating tax.
- Many users are confused about switching regimes at the time of ITR filing vs what the employer used for TDS.
- Lack of clear guides on handling missing Form 16s or discrepancies between Form 16 and AIS.
- Notice period pay and joining bonus taxability are frequent pain points for job switchers.
